Research

First Research

Pill Navigation

The pill navigation, also known as a tabbed navigation, is a common design pattern used in web development. It consists of a series of buttons, usually horizontally arranged, that can be used to navigate between different sections of a website.Upon searching for a suitable navigation bar for the website, the pill navigation which was available in w3school was found to be suitable. So, w3school's pill navigation was taken as reference

Implementation:

After using the W3Schools pill navigation as a reference, customizations was made to better fit the look and feel of website. By changing the colors and adding a logo,a unique visual identity for the website that sets it apart from others was created.

Second Research

Product Page

When looking for making product page design, this website came across the way and its product alignment were selected for the website. By using this design as a reference, product page is visually appealing and presents products in an organized and attractive manner.

Implementation:

After choosing a website as a reference for the product page design, made some simple changes to better fit the needs. By adding a discounted price and a "Add to Cart" button, made it easier for customers to quickly see the cost of the product and purchase it directly from the product page.

Third Research

Blog

While looking for making blog page more appealing, came across slider in w3schools. By using the slider from W3Schools as a reference, ensured that blog page has a professional and effective way of displaying images and content to the users.

Implementation:

That slider was kept in header to make the website more alive with suitable images along with the text in it. The slider serves as a strong focal point and can immediately grab the attention of visitors to the page.

Fourth Research

Footer

While looking for different format or say design of the website, bonsai paradise's footer, this website's footer was found to be appealing. A website's footer is an important part of the design as it provides important information and links to other pages on the site.

Implementation:

After using the footer from Bonsai Paradise as a reference, made several changes to better fit the look and feel of the website. By adjusting the color theme, it was able to create a cohesive and aesthetically pleasing design that is tailored to the specific needs.

Fifth Research

Contact US

When searching for a design for the "Contact Us" form for the website, found the "Send a Message" form on the website 'Himalayan Java' to be appealing. The form had a minimalist design, with input fields that were simple lines. This design is visually appealing and provides a clean and modern look.

Implementation:

Used the minimalist "Send a Message" form from Himalayan Java as a reference for "Contact Us" page and made changes such as adding radio buttons and links to the social media handles. By incorporating these additional elements, was able to provide the visitors with more options and ways to connect.
